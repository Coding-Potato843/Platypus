Add the following image files to this folder:

1. icon.png (1024x1024) - App icon
2. splash.png (1284x2778) - Splash screen image
3. adaptive-icon.png (1024x1024) - Android adaptive icon foreground

You can create simple placeholder images with a cyan (#06b6d4) background
and a platypus emoji or the text "Platypus".

For quick setup, you can use any square image temporarily.
